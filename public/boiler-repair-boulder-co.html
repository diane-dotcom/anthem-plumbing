High-efficiency vs. older boiler

Is it time to replace?

If your boiler is aging or failing often, here's how a modern high-efficiency unit compares to an older one, an honest breakdown.

System factor High-Efficiency Condensing Boiler (Recommended) Older / Standard Boiler
EfficiencyUp to ~95%+ AFUEOften 60-80%, more fuel wasted
Heating billsLower monthly gas useHigher, especially in deep cold
Lifespan15-20+ years with serviceOften near or past end of life
ComfortModulating, even, consistent heatOn/off cycling, uneven warmth
ReliabilityNew parts & warrantyRising repair frequency & cost

Replacing isn't always the answer, a newer boiler with a single failed part is usually worth repairing. We'll weigh the unit's age, repair history, and efficiency, then give you the honest call rather than defaulting to the bigger ticket.
